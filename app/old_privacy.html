4. COOKIE USAGE

4.1. We use cookies and similar technologies such as access tokens so we recognize you when you return to our Services. We use them in different ways such as to authenticate your registration process, ensure site functionality and enable us to provide the services to our SmartUp users.

4.2. When you visit the publicly available sections of SmartUp, a session ID cookie is placed on your computer that only lasts while you’re visiting. We also place persistent cookies (known as a local storage devices) on SmartUp community members’ computers, so that SmartUp remembers them and makes their login experience easier. We may use persistent tracking cookies on your mobile device, mainly for security protection purposes, such as to prevent phishers, scammers, unauthorised log in attempts, and to help you access your hacked account. We do not use any information whilst you are logged off.

4.3. You may set your browser and your mobile settings to block cookies and local storage devices, but if you do so, you may not be able to access the features that SmartUp offers.

4.4. The type of cookie usage includes:

  • those that are strictly necessary to deliver the services and products you have requested, such as, for example purchasing a certain type of licence;
  • cookies related to the performance of SmartUp’s site, such as analytics that help us determine how our site is performing and ensuring a smooth and trouble free experience for our members and visitors;
  • cookies related to the functionality of SmartUp’s site, such as remembering your log in, and third party cookies that also relate to functionality and that you request via social plugins, links to other social networks or viewing of videos

4.5. SmartUp’s cookie functions and explanations below:

  • Analytics and research: SmartUp uses Flurry Analytics to collect information about how visitors use SmartUp. We use the information to compile reports and to help us improve the site. The cookies collect information in an anonymous form, including the number of visitors to the site, where visitors have come to the site from and the pages they visited. For more information about Google’s privacy policy, please visit  http://www.google.com/intl/en/policies/
  • Authentication: These cookies help us to identify our members so that when you’re logged in, you can enjoy SmartUp’s products and services.
  • Security and site integrity: We may use persistent cookies, which will help us to ensure we have identified the same device is logging into the correct account. These types of cookies also help with our anti-spam measures and may help us to prevent phishers, scammers, unauthorised log -in attempts to your account and accessing any hacked accounts.
  • Site features and services: In some cases, the site feature you choose may allow a third party to place cookies or local storage devices on your computer. The third party who places cookies on your device is responsible for how they process their data and SmartUp recommends that you read their privacy policies. Third parties who place cookies on your device when you use SmartUp include (but is not limited to) Branch.io, Flurry Analytics, Facebook, LinkedIn and Google platforms.
  • Performance: We need to use certain cookies and local storage devices to assist with your navigation of our site, ensuring pages load up quickly and respond faster to requests for services.

4.6. Our use of cookies and local share devices may change from time to time. Please see this page and our homepage to be aware of the changes.

4.7. Please look at your browser and mobile device for settings on controlling cookies. We do not control these choices offered by your browser/mobile device makers.
